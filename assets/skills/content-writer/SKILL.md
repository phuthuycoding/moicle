# Content Writer Skill

Build a comprehensive multi-channel content strategy — from audience research and content pillars to SEO blog posts, social media plans, newsletter campaigns, and editorial calendars.

## Workflow

```
RESEARCH → PLAN → WRITE → OPTIMIZE → DISTRIBUTE
```

---

## Phase 1: RESEARCH

**Goal**: Understand audience, competitors, and SEO landscape.

1. **Gather strategy requirements**: brand/product details, unique value proposition, voice/tone, target audience (demographics, pain points, content consumption channels), goals (awareness, SEO traffic, leads, education, community, thought leadership), current content state, available resources, and top 3 competitors' content strengths
2. **Keyword and topic research**: identify target keywords by intent (informational, navigational, commercial) with estimated volume, difficulty, and priority. Perform content gap analysis — topics competitors cover that you don't, topics no one covers well, trending topics

### Gate
- [ ] Audience clearly defined
- [ ] Goals established
- [ ] Keyword research completed
- [ ] Competitor content analyzed
- [ ] Content gaps identified

---

## Phase 2: PLAN

**Goal**: Define content pillars, content types, and editorial plan.

1. **Content pillars**: Define 3-5 core themes all content maps back to, each with a goal (educate, inspire, convert, engage) and topic list
2. **Content tiers**:
   - **Tier 1 — Flagship** (2000-3000 words): SEO authority pieces, in-depth guides
   - **Tier 2 — Supporting** (1000-1500 words): Practical tutorials, how-to guides
   - **Tier 3 — Quick** (500-800 words): Tips, digests, feature spotlights
3. **Social media plan per platform**: Define content mix percentages (educational, product, community, industry), post types and frequency, and format guidelines for each platform (X/Twitter, LinkedIn, Dev.to/Medium)
4. **Newsletter plan**: Name, frequency, platform, section structure, growth strategy (blog CTAs, lead magnets, social promotion)

### Gate
- [ ] 3-5 content pillars defined
- [ ] Content tiers with specific titles planned
- [ ] Social media strategy per platform
- [ ] Newsletter template and schedule set
- [ ] User approved the content plan

---

## Phase 3: WRITE

**Goal**: Draft content pieces following brand voice.

1. **Blog posts**: Write with SEO-optimized title (max 60 chars), meta description (150-160 chars with keyword), proper heading hierarchy (H1 with primary keyword, H2s with related keywords), short paragraphs (2-4 sentences), internal links (2-3), external authority links (1-2), and images with alt text
2. **Social media**: Platform-appropriate formatting — tweets with hook/value/CTA structure, threads for deep dives, LinkedIn posts with pattern-interrupt hooks and engagement CTAs
3. **Newsletters**: Subject line (max 50 chars, curiosity-driven), preview text, personal opening, main story, quick tips section, community highlight, resource links, clear CTA

### Gate
- [ ] Content follows brand voice and tone
- [ ] Blog posts follow SEO structure
- [ ] Social posts follow platform conventions
- [ ] Newsletter follows template

### Feedback Loop
Content needs revision → edit based on feedback → re-review

---

## Phase 4: OPTIMIZE

**Goal**: Review content for SEO, quality, and brand consistency.

1. **SEO check**: Primary keyword in title, first paragraph, at least one H2, and meta description. Keyword density 1-2%. URL slug clean and keyword-rich. Images have descriptive alt tags. Subheadings every 200-300 words
2. **Quality check**: Facts correct and current, code examples tested, screenshots up to date, links valid, no sensitive information, tone matches brand, consistent terminology, no spelling/grammar errors

### Gate
- [ ] SEO checklist passed
- [ ] Quality checklist passed
- [ ] Content approved

---

## Phase 5: DISTRIBUTE

**Goal**: Create distribution calendar and define tracking.

1. **Editorial calendar**: Weekly themes, daily content assignments across platforms, repurposing flow (Blog → Thread → LinkedIn → Newsletter → Cross-post)
2. **KPIs**: Define targets for blog (visitors, page views, time on page, rankings), social (followers growth, engagement rate, impressions, clicks), newsletter (subscribers, open rate, click rate), and conversion (blog→signup, social→website, newsletter→action)
3. **Review cadence**: Weekly social/traffic snapshot, monthly full analysis with top 5 content ranking, quarterly content audit with pillar review and strategy adjustment

### Gate
- [ ] Editorial calendar created
- [ ] KPIs defined with realistic targets
- [ ] Review cadence established
- [ ] User approved distribution plan
